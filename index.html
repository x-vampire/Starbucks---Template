ITEMS WE HAVE


Cold Coffee


Cold Coffee is a line of blended iced coffee drinks sold by Starbucks. It may consist of coffee or crème base, blended with ice and ingredients such as flavored syrups and usually topped with whipped cream and or spices.


Hot Chocolate


Hot chocolate drinks that are perfect for warming up on a cold day. Their most popular hot chocolate is the Signature Hot Chocolate, which is made with steamed milk, chocolate-flavored syrups, and topped with whipped cream and chocolate drizzle.


Frappuccino


The beauty of Frappuccinos lies in their customizability! You can adjust the sweetness, add extra toppings like whipped cream, drizzle, or sprinkles, and even substitute dairy-free milk options like almond or oat milk.


Cappuccino


the cappuccino – a symphony of espresso, steamed milk, and foamy perfection! At Starbucks, this classic Italian coffee beverage gets a modern twist with various customizations and sizes to satisfy your cravings.
